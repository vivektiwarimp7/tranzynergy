Our Mission

Our mission is simple: To provide a business engine and community synergy that enables freelance translators to achieve their desired lifestyle including a path to earn more than their personal production. The problem most freelancers face is the lack of community while working. In an office setting, there’s always an opportunity to bounce ideas and brainstorm with other people. As a freelancer, those opportunities are not as readily available. That’s where we step in. We’re bridging the gap between working alone and working with peers.

How to get started

To become a member of tranZynergy you’ll need the following:

1

Sufficient linguistic skills to make a living as a translator - this is why tranZynergy emphasises specialties and training.

2

A desire to work hard to build your business - this platform won’t hand you clients, but it will make it easier for you to catch them.

3

An ability to implement a proven model for success - we’ve invented a few variations of the wheel so there’s no need for you to reinvent it.
